# Research Clarity Patch

This patch makes research upgrades clearer.

## Changes

- Research buttons now show their Data cost.
- Buttons are disabled when the player cannot afford the upgrade.
- Hover/title text shows how much more Data is needed.
- Insufficient Data now writes a clear Facility Log entry and shows a toast.
- Successful upgrades log the exact Data cost spent.

## Why

Previously, the research buttons could appear to do nothing when the player lacked enough Data.
